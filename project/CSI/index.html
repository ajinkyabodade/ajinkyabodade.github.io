Transforming Vision Into Innovation

The Computer Science Of India , most popularly known as CSI, is the association of Computer Science & Engineering students in PLIT. CSI works with the motto of "Transforming Vision Into Innovation" & focuses on the development of technical skills in the students

The Whole Picture

Formed in 1965, the CSI has been instrumental in guiding the Indian IT industry down the right path since its formative years. Today, the CSI has 72 chapters all over India, 511 student branches, and more than 100000 members including India's most famous IT industry leaders, brilliant scientists and dedicated academicians. This institute has a CSI student branch in 2013 under the supervision of honorable Dr. P. M. Jawandhiya ,Principal ,PLITMS Buldana. His devotion and correspondence towards the technical education leads to the formation of CSI student branch with 75 student members at start all from the undergraduate course. Prof.V.P.Narkhede is been appointed as the Student Branch Counselor and Prof.A.O.Bang is giving his services as a faculty advisor for this student branch. Then as time passed different training programs, short term workshops were held at department of CSE to upgrade the knowledge pool of student. Currently we have totally 86 students who are members of student branch constituting 52 students from undergraduate course and 34 students are from post graduate level.
