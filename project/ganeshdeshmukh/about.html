MY INSPIRATION

My inspiration is my father Late Hon'ble Mr. P. P. Deshmukh [Retired Executive Engineer & Research Officer, Maharashtra Jeevan Pradhikaran(M.J.P.), Navi Mumbai] who has completed his M.Tech. (Env. Engg.) from VNIT Nagpur. He saved crores of Rupees of our nation & serves the rural area of Maharashtra. Inspired from his great contribution, I have initiated this social activity to guide the students of all streams about their study & career.
